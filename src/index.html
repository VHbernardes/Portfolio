Project 1

forUnB is a forum platform inspired by the Stack Overflow concept, created to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing among students at the University of Brasília. Our goal is to build a united community where students can share questions, find answers, and collaborate across various subjects. The platform was developed entirely in Django, with styling implemented using Bootstrap. We leveraged Render for hosting, and PostgreSQL was used as the database. Additionally, we implemented web scraping using BeautifulSoup to gather academic materials from the University of Brasília, enhancing the resources available to users.
